Conclusion

By choosing to use this font pairing, consisting of two fonts, one made for text in small sizes, the other supports the readability of the letters really well, especially in longer text,
I've managed to create a easier readable text, taking into consideration line height, spacing of words and the hierarchy of the fonts.
I've wanted a cleaner look for these two fonts and I think the adamina and aBeeZee font does achieve that.
This is also evident when I utilize the fonts in my UI cards components.
Having the card components grouped in the way that they are,with the law of proximity in mind, gives the content of each card their own space.
